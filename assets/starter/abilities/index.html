Abilities

Abilities are typed functions that plugins (and core, in WP 7.0) register so AI agents can call them. Yoast SEO Premium 27.5 ships abilities like analyze_page_seo; an installed DSGo App can invoke them.

Live: dsgo.abilities.list()

Permission: abilities

Returns the abilities matching this app’s abilities.consumes patterns. This starter consumes wp/* + dsgo/* — if no plugin has registered abilities yet, the list will be empty.

        The pattern
        Abilities turn DSGo Apps into composable agents: write a UI in your app, let the user (or your app’s AI loop) call abilities registered by other plugins. Iframe-mode apps can also publish abilities of their own via abilities.publishes; the site’s AI agent can then call into your app.
